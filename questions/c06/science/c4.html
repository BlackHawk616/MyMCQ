Sorting Materials into Groups

Question 01:

Anything that has a mass and occupies space is called

  • a. matter
  • b. air
  • c. classification
  • d. volume
Answer

Answer : a. matter

Question 02:

The process of grouping the things on the basis of similar properties is called

  • a. arrangement
  • b. classification
  • c. matter
  • d. roughness
Answer

Answer : b. classification

Question 03:

Which gas is responsible for fizz in soft drinks?

  • a. Hydrogen
  • b. Nitrogen
  • c. Carbon dioxide
  • d. Oxygen
Answer

Answer : c. Carbon dioxide

Question 04:

Which of the following is soluble in water?

  • a. Chalk powder
  • b. Mud
  • c. Sand
  • d. Salt
Answer

Answer : d. Salt

Question 05:

Purpose of sorting materials into groups is

  • a. convenience
  • b. to study their properties
  • c. none of these
  • d. both (a) and (b)
Answer

Answer : d. both (a) and (b)

Question 06:

An oily thin paper will be

  • a. translucent
  • b. opaque
  • c. cannot be predicted
  • d. transparent
Answer

Answer : a. translucent

Question 07:

Which of the following gets reddish brown in colour on exposure to air?

  • a. Aluminium
  • b. Iron
  • c. Silver
  • d. Gold
Answer

Answer : b. Iron/p>

Question 08:

An iron nail is kept in each of the following liquids. In which case would it lose its shine and appear dull?

  • a. Mustard oil
  • b. Kerosene
  • c. Soft drink
  • d. Coconut oil
Answer

Answer : a.

Question 09:

Pick one material from the following which is completely soluble in water.

  • a. Chalk powder
  • b. Glucose
  • c. Saw dust
  • d. Tea leaves
Answer

Answer : a.

Question 10:

You are provided with the following materials

(i) Magnifying glass (ii) Mirror (iii) Stainless steel plate (iv) Glass tumbler

Which of the above materials will you identify as transparent?

  • a. (i) and (ii)
  • b. (i) and (iii)
  • c. (iii) and (iv)
  • d. (i) and (iv)
Answer

Answer : a.
