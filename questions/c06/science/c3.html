  • b. shirt
  • c. trousers

Answer

Answer : d. sweater

Question 03:

Cotton fibres are separated from

  • a. all of these

  • c. cotton bales

Answer

Answer : d. cotton boils

Question 04:

Separation of fibres of cotton from its seeds is known as

  • b. spinning

Answer

Answer : a. ginning
