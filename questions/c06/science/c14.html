Food : Where does it come from?

Question 01:

Which of the following is not a food?

  • a. Pulses
  • b. Bread
  • c. Ghee
  • d. Petrol
Answer

Answer : d. Petrol

Question 02:

Food is necessary because

  • a. it provides energy
  • b. it keeps us healthy
  • c. it provides growth
  • d. all of these
Answer

Answer : d. all of these

Question 03:

Given below are names of some animals:

  • (i) Goat (ii) Human beings (iii) Cockroach (iv) Eagle

    • Which of the above animals form a pair of omnivores?

    • a. (ii) and (iv)
    • b. (iii) and (iv)
    • c. (i) and (ii)
    • d. (ii) and (iii)
    Answer

    Answer : a. (ii) and (iv)

    Question 04:

    Honeybee makes honey from

    • a. nectar
    • b. bud
    • c. petals
    • d. pollen
    Answer

    Answer : a. nectar

    Question 05:

    Below are names of some animals:

  • (i) Cow (ii) Sheep (iii) Horse (iv) Ox

    • Which of the above are sources of milk for human beings?

    • b. (iii) and (iv)
    • c. (i) and (iii)
    • d. (i) and (ii)

    Answer : d. (i) and (ii)
