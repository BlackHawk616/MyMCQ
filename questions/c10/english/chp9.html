Madam Rides the Bus

Question 01:

What was Valli’s age?
  • a. 5
  • b. 10
  • c. 7
  • d. 8
Answer

Answer : d. 8

Question 02:

How many times did the bus cross her street per hour?
  • a. 2
  • b. 4
  • c. 6
  • d. 8
Answer

Answer : a. 2

Question 03:

“Valli would stare wistfully at the people who got on or off the bus.” What is the meaning of wistfully?
  • a. longingly
  • b. willingly
  • c. carefully
  • d. fearfully
Answer

Answer : a: longingly

Question 04:

How did she pick up small details about the bus journey?
  • a. asking a few discreet questions
  • b. listening to conversations of neighbours
  • c. All of the above
  • d. None of the above
Answer

Answer : c. All of the above

Question 05:

What did the old man say to Valli that annoyed her?
  • a. he asked why she’s alone
  • b. he merely asked her to sit
  • c. he shouted at her
  • d. None of the above
Answer

Answer : b. he merely asked her to sit

Question 06:

Why did Vali find the woman repulsive?
  • a. she wore ugly earrings
  • b. she was chewing betel nut which could spill out any moment
  • c. she had large piercings in her ears
  • d. all of the above
Answer

Answer : d. all of the above

Question 07:

What did she have to resist on Village Fair Day to save for the ride?
  • a. peppermint
  • b. toys
  • c. merry go round
  • d. balloons
Answer

Answer : c. merry go round

Question 08:

What made her laugh on her journey?
  • a. the old woman’s earrings
  • b. the sight of a running cow
  • c. the old man
  • d. None of the above
Answer

Answer : b. the sight of a running cow

Question 09:

What was the timing of the afternoon nap taken by Valli’s mother?
  • a. 1 to 2
  • b. 1 to 4
  • c. 2 to 5
  • d. 1 to 3
Answer

Answer : b. 1 to 4

Question 10:

What can you tell about the conductor from the text?
  • a. he was quiet
  • b. he was grumpy
  • c. he was funny
  • d. all of the above
Answer

Answer : c. he was funny
