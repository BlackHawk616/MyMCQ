Question 12:

Extract

When he finished, he went to the window to buy a stamp which he licked and then affixed to the envelope with a blow of his fist. The moment the letter fell into the mailbox, the postmaster went to open it. It said: “God: Of the money that I asked for, only seventy pesos reached me. Send me the rest, since I need it very much. But don’t send it to me through the mail because the post office employees are a bunch of crooks. Lencho.”

I) What was the most likely response that the postmaster expected in Lencho’s second letter?
  • (i) Sorrowful
  • (ii) Disappointment
  • (iii) Gratitude
  • (iv) Shock
  • (v) Elation

  • a. i and iii
  • b. ii and v
  • c. ii and iv
  • d. iii and v
Answer

Answer: d. (iii) & (v)

II) Lencho’s letter included …………………..
  • a. Details of his problems
  • b. List of further demands
  • c. Gratitude
  • d. Description of the post office
Answer

Answer: b. List of further demands

III) ‘______ bunch of crooks.’ Pick the option that DOES NOT collate with ‘bunch of’ correctly.
  • (i) bunch of keys
  • (ii) bunch of islands
  • (iii) bunch of grapes
  • (iv) bunch of flowers

  • a. Option (ii)
  • b. Option (iii)
  • c. Option (iv)
  • d. Option (i)
Answer

Answer: a. Option (ii)

IV) Pick the most suitable quote for this extract.
  • a. “Real knowledge is to know the extent of one’s ignorance.”- Confucius
  • b. “True generosity means accepting ingratitude.” – Coco Chanel
  • c. “It is easier to fool people than to convince them that they have been fooled.” – Mark Twain
