How to Tell Wild Animals

Question 01:

What two things does a Chameleon not have?
  • a. does not have ears and wings.
  • b. does not have nose and wings.
  • c. does not have ears and nose.
  • d. none of the above.
Answer

Answer : a. does not have ears and wings.

Question 02:

How is the leopard’s skin?
  • a. has holes on his skin.
  • b. has spots on his skin.
  • c. has stripes on his skin.
  • d. none of the above.
Answer

Answer : b. has spots on his skin.

Question 03:

Where is the Chameleon generally found?
  • a. In the land.
  • b. In the sky.
  • c. In the trees.
  • d. In the Garden
Answer

Answer : c. In the trees.

Question 04:

What is the special feature of the Bengal Tiger?
  • a. black stripes on his yellow body.
  • b. white stripes on his yellow body.
  • c. black stripes on his white body.
  • d. red stripes on his yellow body.
Answer

Answer : a. black stripes on his yellow body.

Question 05:

How does a leopard behave when he meets a person?
  • a. he starts crying.
  • b. he try to frighten the person.
  • c. he jumps on him at once.
  • d. he run towards the person.
Answer

Answer : c. he jumps on him at once.

Question 06:

What is the difference between a chameleon and a lizard?

  • b. Chameleon doesn’t have ears and wings
  • c. Chameleons doesn’t have wings
  • d. Lizards don’t have wings
