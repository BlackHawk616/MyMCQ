The Hundred Dressess - 2

Question 01:

Who wrote the letter received by Miss Mason?
  • a. Wanda’s brother
  • b. Wanda’s father
  • c. Wanda’s mother
  • d. Wanda
Answer

Answer : b. Wanda’s father

Question 02:

What else was mentioned in the letter?
  • a. her medal
  • b. the school
  • c. her funny name
  • d. her drawings
Answer

Answer : c. her funny name

Question 03:

How would you describe Miss Mason’s feelings towards the class?
  • a. Happy
  • b. Angry
  • c. Cheerful
  • d. Upset
Answer

Answer : d. Upset

Question 04:

What did Maddie think of herself after the letter was read out loud?
  • a. Coward
  • b. Lucky
  • c. Hero
  • d. Grateful
Answer

Answer : a. Coward

Question 05:

What was Maddie reminded of by the Petronski’s house?
  • a. Wanda’s dress; unironed but clean
  • b. Wanda’s drawings
  • c. how she could stop all of it
  • d. how they teased her
Answer

Answer : a. Wanda’s dress; unironed but clean

Question 06:

What important decision does Maddie take?
  • a. visiting Wanda
  • b. stop others from bullying
  • c. Unfriending Peggy
  • d. all of the above
Answer

Answer : b. stop others from bullying

Question 07:

What did they write in the letter?
  • a. Her drawings and that she won
  • b. apology
  • c. both 1 and 2
  • d. asked about new city
Answer

Answer : c. both 1 and 2

Question 08:

What was the tone of Wanda’s reply?
  • a. hatred
  • b. ungrateful
  • c. Angry
  • d. Friendly
Answer

Answer : d. Friendly

Question 09:

What was Maddie’s reaction on receiving the drawing?
  • a. happy
  • b. sad
  • c. neutral
  • d. excited
Answer

Answer : b. sad

Question 10:

What was the description of the house which Maddie thought to be Wanda’s house?
  • a. Small green cottage
  • b. Big white house
  • c. Tiny Red hut
  • d. Little white house
Answer

Answer : d. Little white house
