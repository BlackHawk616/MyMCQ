A Tiger in the Zoo

Question 01:

Name the poet of the poem ‘A Tiger in the Zoo’.
  • a. Walt Whitman
  • b. Carolyn Wells
  • c. Robert Frost
  • d. Leslie Norris
Answer

Answer : d. Leslie Norris

Question 02:

The poem draws a contrast between _______ and _______.
  • a. tiger in a zoo, humans
  • b. humans, tiger in forest
  • c. tiger in a zoo, tiger in a forest
  • d. animals, human beings
Answer

Answer : a. tiger in a zoo, humans

Question 03:

What has been personified in the poem?
  • a. Zoo
  • b. Tiger
  • c. Forest
  • d. All of these
Answer

Answer : b. Tiger

Question 04:

Why should he be lurking in shadow?
  • a. Out of anger
  • b. To catch the deer
  • c. Out of fear
  • d. Both (a) & (c).
Answer

Answer : d. Both (a) & (c).

Question 05:

What describes ‘tiger in a cell’?
  • a. Snarling around houses
  • b. Baring his white fangs
  • c. Locked in concrete cell
  • d. Shadow, long grass
Answer

Answer : c. Locked in concrete cell

Question 06:

By‘ignoring visitors’, what is the poet trying to say?
  • a. He is less terrorising because of the cage
  • b. Tiger knows his power is restricted
  • c. There is no use of showing rage
  • d. All of the above
Answer

Answer : d. All of the above

Question 07:

The tiger is locked in a/an:
  • a. concrete cell
  • b. village house
  • c. open cell
  • d. wooden room
Answer

Answer : a. concrete cell

Question 08:

Name the poetic device used in the line “On pads of velvet quiet”.
  • a. Assonance
  • b. Consonance
  • c. Metaphor
  • d. Oxymoron
Answer

Answer : c. Metaphor

Question 09:

The tiger hears the voice of at night.
  • a. birds
  • b. patrolling cars
  • c. other jungle animals
  • d. guards
Answer

Answer : b. patrolling cars

Question 10:

What described ‘tiger in a jungle’?
  • a. Ignoring visitors
  • b. Baring his white fangs
  • c. Locked in concrete cell
  • d. His strength behind bars .
Answer

Answer : b. Baring his white fangs
