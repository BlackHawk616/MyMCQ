The Sermon at Benares

Question 01:

Where did Buddha preach his first sermon?
  • a. Patliputra
  • b. Chennai
  • c. Haridwar
  • d. Benares
Answer

Answer : d. Benares

Question 02:

What is the world afflicted with?
  • a. Diseases
  • b. Death and decay
  • c. Love
  • d. None of the Above
Answer

Answer : b. Death and decay

Question 03:

What does the ripe fruit fear?
  • a. They will rot
  • b. Someone will eat them
  • c. Falling down
  • d. None of the Above
Answer

Answer : c. Falling down

Question 04:

Where did Budhha sit?
  • a. Deodar cedar
  • b. Mango Tree
  • c. Fig Tree
  • d. Peepal Tree
Answer

Answer : c. Fig Tree

Question 05:

According to Buddha, what is an inscrutable kind of suffering?
  • a. beating
  • b. loss of a loved one
  • c. verbal abuse
  • d. hatred
Answer

Answer : b. loss of a loved one

Question 06:

Those who do not grieve are _____.
  • a. arrogant
  • b. happy
  • c. proud
  • d. wise
Answer

Answer : d. wise

Question 07:

Who is free from sorrow?
  • a. who overcomes sorrow
  • b. who remains happy
  • c. who gives sorrow to others
  • d. who does not see sorrow
Answer

Answer : a. who overcomes sorrow

Question 08:

What did Kisa ask for to bring her son back to life?
  • a. medicine
  • b. prayers
  • c. doctor
  • d. money
Answer

Answer : a. medicine

Question 09:

He got enlightenment after how many days?
  • a. 28
  • b. 21
  • c. 14
  • d. 7
Answer

Answer : d. 7

Question 10:

What does Kisa search for from house to house in the first place?
  • a. pumpkin seeds
  • b. mustard seed
  • c. a medicine to bring her son back to life
  • d. None of the above
Answer

Answer : c. a medicine to bring her son back to life
