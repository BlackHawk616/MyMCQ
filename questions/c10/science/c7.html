Control And Coordination

Question 01:

Manish’s mother was baking cake in the kitchen. When Manish came back from school, he detected smell of hot cake from the drawing room. Why?
  • a. Due to the presence of olfactory receptors in forebrain
  • b. Due to the presence of taste buds
  • c. Due to the presence of olfactory receptors in midbrain
  • d. Due to the presence of olfactory receptors in hindbrain
Answer

Answer : a. Due to the presence of olfactory receptors in forebrain

Question 02:

How will information travel within a neuron?
  • a. Dendrite -> cell body -> axon -> nerve ending
  • b. Dendrite -> axon -> cell body -> nerve ending
  • c. Axon -> dendrite -> cell body -> nerve ending
  • d. Axon -> cell body -> dendrite -> nerve ending
Answer

Answer : a. Dendrite -> cell body -> axon -> nerve ending

Question 03:

Which parts of the brain control blood pressure?
  • a. Spinal cord, skull, hypothalamus
  • b. Cord, skull, cerebrum
  • c. Pons, medulla, pituitary
  • d. Pons, medulla, cerebellum
Answer

Answer : d. Pons, medulla, cerebellum

Question 04:

Which plant hormone promotes dormancy in seeds and buds?
  • a. gibberellin
  • b. cytokinin
  • c. abscisic acid
  • d. auxin
Answer

Answer : c. abscisic acid

Question 05:

Which plant hormone promotes cell division?
  • a. abscisic acid
  • b. cytokinin
  • c. auxin
  • d. gibberellins
Answer

Answer : b. cytokinin

Question 06:

A part of the body which responds to the in-structions sent from nervous system is called
  • a. nerves
  • b. receptor
  • c. effector
  • d. muscles
Answer

Answer : c. effector

Question 07:

A microscopic gap between a pair of adjacent neurons over which nerve impulses pass is called
  • a. axon
  • b. guard cells
  • c. synapse
  • d. dendrites
Answer

Answer : c. synapse

Question 08:

The main function of cerebrum is
  • a. balancing
  • b. thinking
  • c. memory
  • d. hearing
Answer

Answer : b. thinking

Question 09:

Identify which of the following statements about thyroxin is incorrect?
  • a. Thyroid gland requires iodine to synthesize thyroxin.
  • b. Thyroxin is also called thyroid hormone.
  • c. It regulates protein, carbohydrates and fat metabolism in the body.
  • d. Iron is essential for the synthesis of thyroxin.
Answer

Answer : d. Iron is essential for the synthesis of thyroxin.
