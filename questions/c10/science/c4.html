Carbon And Its Compounds

Question 01:

What is the difference in the molecular mass of any two adjacent homologues?
  • a. 13 amu
  • b. 15 amu
  • c. 14 amu
  • d. 17 amu
Answer

Answer : c. 14 amu

Question 02:

Structural formulae of X and Y, which have the same molecular formula, C3H60 are :
  • a. X = CH3CH2CHO, Y= CH3COCH3
  • b. X = CH3OCH2CH3, Y= CH3COCH3
  • c. X = CH3COCH3, Y = CH3COOH
  • d. X= CH3CH2OCH3, Y = CH3CH2COOH
Answer

Answer : a. X = CH3CH2CHO, Y= CH3COCH3

Question 03:

Why are ionic compounds hard crystalline solids?
  • a. Due to the strong force of attraction between positive ions.
  • b. Due to the strong force of attraction between negative ions.
  • c. Due to strong force of attraction between molecules.
  • d. Both (a) and (b)
Answer

Answer : d. Both (a) and (b)

Question 04:

The electronic configuration of an element is found to be 2, 4. How many bonds can one carbon atom form in a compound?
  • a. 4
  • b. 2
  • c. 3
  • d. 6
Answer

Answer : a. 4

Question 05:

A carbon compound contains two atoms of carbon. Which name should the carbon compound bear?
  • a. methane
  • b. ethane
  • c. propane
  • d. butane
Answer

Answer : b. ethane

Question 06:

A student studies that vinegar, which is a diluted form of ethanoic acid, freezes during winter. What does this suggest about the physical properties of pure ethanoic acid?
  • a. It has a low boiling point
  • b. It has a very high melting point
  • c. It has a very high boiling point
  • d. It has a low melting point
Answer

Answer : d. It has a low melting point

Question 07:

Which of the following is the molecular formula of cyclobutane?
  • a. C4H10
  • b. C4H6
  • c. C4H8
  • d. C4H4
Answer

Answer : c. C4H8

Question 08:

How many single bonds are present in methane?
  • a. 1
  • b. 4
  • c. 6
  • d. 3
Answer

Answer : b. 4

Question 09:

A student studies that a soap molecule has two ends, one of which is an ionic end, and the other is the carbonic chain. Which option explains the interaction of a soap molecule with oil?
  • a. Carbonic chain end of the soap interacts with the oil
  • b. Ends of the soap randomly interact with the oil
  • c. The closest end of the soap interacts with the oil
  • d. Ionic end of the soap interacts with the oil
Answer

Answer : a. Carbonic chain end of the soap interacts with the oil

Question 10:

Which of the following belongs to a homologous series of alkynes?
  • a. C2H4
  • b. C6H6
  • c. C3H4
  • d. C2H6
Answer

Answer : c. C3H4
