Heridity And Evolution

Question 01:

A pea plant is represented by Rr. This represents the
  • a. Alternate form of genes
  • b. Characteristics which are visible in an organism.
  • c. Genetic composition of an individual
  • d. None of these
Answer

Answer : c. Genetic composition of an individual

Question 02:

Two pea plants one with round green seeds (RRyy) and another with wrinkled yellow (rrYY) seeds produce F1 progeny that have round yellow (RrYy) seeds. When F1 plants are selfpollinated, the F2 progeny will have new combination of characters. Choose the new combination from the following
  • 1. Round, yellow
  • 2. Round, green
  • 3. Wrinkled, yellow
  • 4. Wrinkled, green

  • a. (i) and (ii)
  • b. (ii) and (iii)
  • c. (i) and (iv)
  • d. (i) and (iii)
Answer

Answer : c. (i) and (iv)

Question 03:

In a study it was found that fused ear lobes were found in more numbers within a population rather than free ear lobes. What can you infer from the above observation with respect to dominant/ recessive trait?
  • a. Fused ear lobes – dominant
  • b. Free ear lobes – dominant
  • c. Fused ear lobes – recessive
  • d. Both are dominant
Answer

Answer : a. Fused ear lobes – dominant

Question 04:

Which section of DNA provides information for one protein?
  • a. Trait
  • b. Nucleus
  • c. Chromosome
  • d. Gene
Answer

Answer : d. Gene

Question 05:

In peas, a pure tall (TT) is crossed with a pure short plant(tt). The ratio of pure tall plants to pure short plants in the F2 generation is:
  • a. 1:1
  • b. 2:1
  • c. 3:1
  • d. 1:3
Answer

Answer : a. 1:1

Question 06:

Which of the following is a pair of analogous organs?
  • a. Forelimbs of a rabbit and a lizard
  • b. Wings of a pigeon and a bat
  • c. Leaves of a pitcher plant and a Venus fly trap
  • d. Forelimbs of a frog and a bird
Answer

Answer : b. Wings of a pigeon and a bat

Question 07:

Two pink-coloured flowers on crossing resulted in 1 red, 2 pink and 1 white-flower progeny. The nature of the cross will be:
  • a. self-pollination
  • b. no fertilization
  • c. double fertilization
  • d. cross-fertilization
Answer

Answer : d. cross-fertilization

Question 08:

What is the probability that the male progeny will be a boy?
  • a. 50 %
  • b. 47.43 %
  • c. 56 %
  • d. It varies
Answer

Answer : a. 50 %

Question 09:

Which of the following does Darwin’s theory not include?
  • a. Struggle for the existence of life
  • b. Natural Selection
  • c. Evolution Through Inheritance
  • d. Survival of the fittest
Answer

Answer : c. Evolution Through Inheritance

Question 10:

Exchange of genetic material takes place in:
  • a. Asexual reproduction
  • b. Sexual reproduction
  • c. Budding
  • d. Vegetative reproduction
