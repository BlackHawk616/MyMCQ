Our Environment

Question 01:

The amount of energy that flows from one trophic level to another in a food chain is
  • a. 15%
  • b. 20 %
  • c. 5%
  • d. 10 %
Answer

Answer : d. 10 %

Question 02:

In a food chain, the third trophic level is always occupied by
  • a. herbivores
  • b. producers
  • c. carnivores
  • d. decomposers
Answer

Answer : c. carnivores

Question 03:

In the given food chain, suppose the amount of energy at fourth trophic level is 5 kJ, what will be the energy available at the producer level? Grass → Grasshopper → Frog → Snake → Hawk
  • a. 5000 k J
  • b. 500 k J
  • c. 5 k J
  • d. 50 k J
Answer

Answer : a. 5000 k J

Question 04:

In an ecosystem, the 10% of energy available for transfer from one trophic level to the next is in the form of
  • a. light energy
  • b. chemical energy
  • c. heat energy
  • d. mechanical energy
Answer

Answer : b. chemical energy

Question 05:

The decomposers in ecosystem:
  • a. do not breakdown organic compounds
  • b. convert organic material to inorganic forms
  • c. convert inorganic materials to simpler forms
  • d. none
Answer

Answer : c. convert inorganic materials to simpler forms

Question 06:

The primary consumers are
  • a. Omnivores
  • b. Producers
  • c. Carnivores
  • d. Herbivores
Answer

Answer : d. Herbivores

Question 07:

Which of the following limits the number of trophic levels in a food chain?
  • a. Polluted air and water
  • b. Deficient food supply
  • c. Decrease in energy at higher trophic levels
  • d. Water
Answer

Answer : c. Decrease in energy at higher trophic levels

Question 08:

Flow of energy in an ecosystem is always
  • a. bidirectional
  • b. unidirectional
  • c. multi directional
  • d. no specific direction
Answer

Answer : b. unidirectional

Question 09:

Which of the following is the terrestrial ecosystem?
  • a. pond
  • b. natural forest
  • c. Aquarium
  • d. lake
Answer

Answer : b. natural forest

Question 10:

The percentage of solar radiation absorbed by all the green plants for the process of photosynthesis is about
  • a. 5%
  • b. 10%
  • c. 1%
  • d. 8%
Answer

Answer : c. 1%
