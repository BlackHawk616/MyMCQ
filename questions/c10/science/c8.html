How Do Organisms Reproduce?

Question 01:

Which of the following diseases is transmitted sexually?
  • a. Kala Azar
  • b. Syphilis
  • c. Cholera
  • d. Jaundice
Answer

Answer : b. Syphilis

Question 02:

IUCD is for
  • a. Vegetative propagation
  • b. Avoiding miscarriage
  • c. Increasing fertility
  • d. Contraception
Answer

Answer : d. Contraception

Question 03:

Which event will likely occur in the ovaries of females after attaining puberty?
  • a. production of eggs
  • b. fertilisation
  • c. growth and development of the embryo
  • d. synthesis of eggs
Answer

Answer : a. production of eggs

Question 04:

During favourable conditions, Amoeba reproduces by
  • a. multiple fission
  • b. binary fission
  • c. budding
  • d. fragmentation
Answer

Answer : b. binary fission

Question 05:

The ability of a cell to divide into several cells during reproduction in Plasmodium is called
  • a. reduction division
  • b. budding
  • c. multiple fission
  • d. binary fission
Answer

Answer : c. multiple fission

Question 06:

Bryophyllum can be propagated vegetatively by the
  • a. leaf
  • b. flower
  • c. root
  • d. stem
Answer

Answer : a. leaf

Question 07:

Spirogyra reproduce by
  • a. fission
  • b. regeneration
  • c. fragmentation
  • d. budding
Answer

Answer : c. fragmentation

Question 08:

The period during adolescence when the reproductive tissues begin to mature is called
  • a. ovyfetion
  • b. puberty
  • c. germination
  • d. propagation
Answer

Answer : b. puberty

Question 09:

  • a. Surgery

  • c. Copper-T

Answer : d. condoms

Question 10:

The correct sequence of reproductive stages seen in flowering plants is :
  • a. Gametes, embryo, zygote, seedling
  • b. Zygote, gametes, embryo, seedling
  • c. Seedling, embryo, zygote, gametes
  • d. Gametes, zygote, embryo, seedling
Answer

Answer : d. Gametes, zygote, embryo, seedling
