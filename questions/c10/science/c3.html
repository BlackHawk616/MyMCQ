Metals And Non-Metals

Question 01:

A non-metal used to preserve food material is:
  • a. Carbon
  • b. Nitrogen
  • c. Phosphorus
  • d. Sulphur
Answer

Answer : b. Nitrogen

Question 02:

The arrangement for Copper, Tin, Lead and Mercury, according to the reactivity series, is:
  • a. Lead> Copper> Mercury> Tin
  • b. Mercury> Tin> Lead> Copper
  • c. Tin> Lead> Copper> Mercury
  • d. Copper> Mercury> Tin> Lead
Answer

Answer : c. Tin> Lead> Copper> Mercury

Question 03:

Aluminium is used for making cooking utensils. Which of the following properties of aluminium are responsible for the same?
  • 1. Good thermal conductivity.
  • 2. Good electrical conductivity
  • 3. Ductility
  • 4. High melting point

  • a. (i) and (ii)
  • b. (i) and (iii)
  • c. (ii) and (iii)
  • d. (i) and (iv)
Answer

Answer : d. (i) and (iv)

Question 04:

Which of the following options gives the process of extraction of mercury from its ore cinnabar?
  • a. Cinnabar to convert it into mercuric oxide and then heating it again
  • b. Cooling cinnabar in the presence of excess air
  • c. Cinnabar in the presence of limited air, and then adding a small amount of water
  • d. Cooling cinnabar to convert it into mercuric oxide and then heating it
Answer

Answer : a. Cinnabar to convert it into mercuric oxide and then heating it again

Question 05:

The metals that float when treated with water are:
  • a. Manganese and sodium
  • b. Magnesium and calcium
  • c. Sodium and calcium
  • d. Magnesium and sodium
Answer

Answer : b. Magnesium and calcium

Question 06:

Aqua regia is a freshly prepared mixture of concentrated HNO3 and concentrated HCl in the ratio of:
  • a. 3:2, respectively
  • b. 3:1, respectively
  • c. 2:3, respectively
  • d. 1:3, respectively
Answer

Answer : d. 3:2, respectively

Question 07:

When a non-metal is allowed to react with water:
  • a. H2 gas is formed
  • b. Product formed depends on the temperature
  • c. CO2 gas is formed
  • d. No products are formed
Answer

Answer : d. No products are formed

Question 08:

Which of the following can undergo a chemical reaction?
  • a. ZnSO4 + Fe
  • b. MgSO4 + Pb
  • c. CuSO4 + Fe
  • d. MgSO4 + Fe
Answer

Answer : c. CuSO4 + Fe

Question 09:

The poorest conductor of heat among metals is
  • a. Sodium
  • b. Lead
  • c. Calcium
  • d. Mercury
Answer

Answer : b. Lead

Question 10:

Which of the following pairs will give dis-placement reactions?
  • a. CuSO4 solution and Silver metal
  • b. NaCl solution and Copper metal
  • c. FeSO4 solution and Copper metal
  • d. AgNO3 solution and Copper metal
Answer

Answer : d. AgNO3 solution and Copper metal

  • a. Sonorousness
