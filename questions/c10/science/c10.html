Light, Reflection And Refraction

Question 01:

If an object is placed symmetrically between two plane mirrors, inclined at an angle of 72 degrees, then the total no. of images formed is:
  • a. 2
  • b. infinite
  • c. 5
  • d. 4
Answer

Answer : d. 4

Question 02:

Which of the following can make a parallel beam of light when light from point source is incident to it?
  • a. Two plane mirrors are placed at 90degree to each other
  • b. Concave mirror as well as convex lens
  • c. Concave mirror as well as concave lens
  • d. Convex mirror as well as concave lens
Answer

Answer : b. Concave mirror as well as convex lens

Question 03:

The image which is formed behind the mirror
  • a. partial image
  • b. real image
  • c. blue image
  • d. virtual image
Answer

Answer : d. virtual image

Question 04:

A beam of light incident on a plane mirror forms a real image on reflection. The incident beam is:
  • a. parallel
  • b. divergent
  • c. convergent
  • d. not certain
Answer

Answer : c. convergent

Question 05:

An object is placed at a distance of 40cm in front of a concave mirror of a focal length of 20 cm.
  • a. real, inverted and of the same size as that of the object
  • b. virtual and inverted
  • c. real and erect
  • d. real, inverted and of the opposite size as that of the object
Answer

Answer : a. real, inverted and of the same size as that of the object

Question 06:

A ray of light is incident on a plane mirror at an angle of incidence 300 . The ray after reflection is deviated through :
  • a. 30 degree
  • b. 120 degree
  • c. 90 degree
  • d. 60 degree
Answer

Answer : b. 120 degree

Question 07:

The focal length of a plano-convex lens having a radius of curvature of 10 cm for convex surface and a refractive index of 1.5 will be :
  • a. 5 cm
  • b. 10 cm
  • c. 20 cm
  • d. 15 cm
Answer

Answer : c. 20 cm

Question 08:

A student conducts an experiment using a convex lens. He places the object at a distance of 60 cm in front of the lens and observes that the image is formed at a distance of 30 cm behind the lens. What is the power of the lens?
  • a. 5 dioptre
  • b. 0.005 dioptre
  • c. 50 dioptre
  • d. 0.05 dioptre
Answer

Answer : a. 5 dioptre

Question 09:

You are given water,mustard oil,glycerine and kerosine.In which of these media a ray of light incident obliquely at same angle would bend the most?
  • a. Mustard oil
  • b. Kerosene
  • c. Glycerine
  • d. Water
Answer

Answer : c. Glycerine

Question 10:

A 10 mm long awl pin is placed vertically in front pin is formed at 30cm in front of the mirror. The focal length of this mirror is:
  • a. -30 cm
  • b. -20 cm
  • c. -40 cm
  • d. -60 cm
Answer

Answer : b. -20 cm
