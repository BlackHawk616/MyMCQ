Question 01:

Magnesium ribbon is rubbed before burning because it has a coating of
  • a. Basic magnesium oxide
  • b. Basic magnesium sulphide
  • c. Basic magnesiun carbonate
  • d. Basic magnesium chloride
Answer

Answer : a. Basic magnesium oxide

Question 02:

0xidation is a process of
  • a. reduction of oxygen
  • b. addition of oxygen
  • c. addition of hydrogen
  • d. reduction of hydrogen
Answer

Answer : b. addition of oxygen

Question 03:

Naveen appeared in class test where he wrote some statements but he confused to know the incorrect statement. Would you help him to know that?
  • a. All the decomposition reaction are endothermic reactions.
  • b. The decomposition of calcium carbonate is an endothermic reaction.
  • c. Photosynthesis is an endothermic reaction.
  • d. Electrolysis of water form hydrogen and oxygen is an exothermic reaction.
Answer

Answer : d. Electrolysis of water form hydrogen and oxygen is an exothermic reaction.

Question 04:

The following reaction is used for the preparation of oxygen gas in the laboratory. Which of the following statements (s) is/are correct about the reaction? 2KClO3 (s) + Heat/ Catalyst → 2KCl(s) + 302 (g)
  • a. It is a decomposition reaction and endothermic in nature.
  • b. It is a combination reaction.
  • c. It is a decomposition reaction and accompanied by release of heat.
  • d. It is a photochemical decomposition reaction and exothermic in nature.
Answer

Answer : a. It is a decomposition reaction and endothermic in nature.

Question 05:

Which of the following gases can be used for storage of fresh sample of an oil for a long time?
  • a. Carbon dioxide or oxygen
  • b. Nitrogen or oxygen
  • c. Carbon dioxide or helium
  • d. Helium or nitrogen
Answer

Answer : d. Helium or nitrogen

Question 06:

Which among the following (are) double displacement reaction (s)?
  • a. Pb + CuCl2 → PbCl2 + Cu, and CH4 + 2O2 → CO2 + 2H2O
  • b. Na2SO4 + BaCl2 → BaSO4 + 2NaCl
  • c. Pb + CuCl2 → PbCl2 + Cu, and Na2SO4 + BaCl2 → BaSO4 + 2NaCl
  • d. C + O2 → CO2, and CH4 + 2O2 → CO2 + 2H2O
Answer

Answer : b. Na2SO4 + BaCl2 → BaSO4 + 2NaCl

Question 07:

What type of chemical reactions take place when electricity is passed through water?
  • a. Displacement reaction
  • b. Combination reaction
  • c. Decomposition reaction
  • d. Double displacement reaction
Answer

Answer : a. Displacement reaction

Question 08:

The condition produced by aerial oxidation of fats and oils in foods marked by unpleasant smell and taste is called:
  • a. antioxidation
  • b. reduction
  • c. rancidity
  • d. corrosion
Answer

Answer : c. rancidity

Question 09:

In the given reaction : ZnO + C → Zn + CO.
  • 1. ZnO is being oxidised.
  • 2. CO is being reduced.
  • 3. C is being oxidised.
  • 4. ZnO is being reduced.

Choose the correct statement.

  • a. I and II only
  • b. III and IV only
  • c. I, II, and III only
  • d. All of these
Answer

Answer : b. III and IV only

Question 10:

Why do we store silver chloride in dark-coloured bottles?
  • a. To prevent precipitation of silver chloride
  • b. To prevent decomposition of silver chloride
  • c. To promote decomposition of silver chloride
  • d. All of these
Answer

Answer : b. To prevent decomposition of silver chloride
