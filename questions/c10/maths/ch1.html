Question 01:

HCF of 21, 23, 25 is
  • a. 1
  • b. 3
  • c. 5
  • d. 7
Answer

Answer : a. 1

Question 02:

The decimal expansion of 22/7 is
  • a. Non-terminating and repeating
  • b. Terminating
  • c. None of the above
  • d. Non-terminating and Non-repeating
Answer

Answer : d. Non-terminating and Non-repeating

Question 03:

HCF of 45 and 105 is:
  • a. 5
  • b. 3
  • c. 9
  • d. 15
Answer d. 15

Question 04:

The multiplication of two irrational numbers is
  • a. rational
  • b. irrational
  • c. rational or irrational
  • d. complex
Answer

Answer : c. rational or irrational

Question 05:

The least number that is divisible by all the numbers from 1 to 5 is
  • a. 60
  • b. 70
  • c. 65
  • d. 90
Answer

Answer : a. 60
